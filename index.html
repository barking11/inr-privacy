Intelli Remote Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Intelli Remote (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, and discloses information, and what choices you have with respect to the information.

Information we collect

We collect information about you when you use Intelli Remote. This information includes:

  • Information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, and payment information.
  • Information about your use of Intelli Remote, such as the features you use, the actions you take, and the time, frequency, and duration of your activities.
  • Information we receive from third-party sources, such as advertising and analytics partners, which may include demographic information, device information, and other information about you.
  • Information we collect automatically when you use Intelli Remote, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and the pages you visit before and after using Intelli Remote.

How we use information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve Intelli Remote, and to develop new products and services. Specifically, we use the information to:

  • Provide and improve Intelli Remote, such as by personalizing content and features, and by tracking and analyzing usage and performance.
  • Respond to your inquiries and fulfill your requests, such as by providing customer support and processing payments.
  • Communicate with you about Intelli Remote, such as by sending you updates, alerts, and promotional messages.
  • Protect against, identify, and prevent fraud and other unlawful activity, claims, and other liabilities.
  • Comply with applicable legal requirements and industry standards.

How we share information

We may share the information we collect with:

  • Service providers that perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, data analysis, email delivery, hosting, and other services.
  • Partners with whom we jointly offer products and services, such as integrations.
  • Third-party advertising and analytics partners, who may use the information to serve targeted advertisements to you and to measure the effectiveness of advertisements.
  • Other users of Intelli Remote, such as when you use features that allow you to share content or communicate with other users.
  • Law enforcement, government agencies, and other third parties, as required by law or as necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Your choices

You can control your information and how it is used in several ways:

  • You can access and update your account information and preferences at any time by logging into your account and changing your settings.
  • You can opt out of receiving promotional messages from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in those messages.
  • You can control cookies and other tracking technologies through your browser settings and other tools.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on our website. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting.
